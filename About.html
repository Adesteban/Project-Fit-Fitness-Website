Project Fit Fitness Center

HISTORY (2012-2021)

Dinno Hilario, the founder of the gym, first opened Project Fit Fitness Center in November 16,2012, Obando, Bulacan. At first, it was small that only five persons are allowed to enter the gym. But as the gym gains more attraction due to its modern weightlifting equipments, people from Obando started to invite their family and friends. The gym was built with modern design, it was located in 3201 Paliwas Street Obando, Bulacan at 4th floor of Obando's Fisherman building.





Our Commitment to Equity

PROJECT'S MISSION

Racism has no place in our gym or our communities – both in the Philippines and around the globe. At Project Fit, we don’t care what color your skin is. We don’t care where you were born.
